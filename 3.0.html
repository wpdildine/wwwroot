Three Levels of Floortime Play

Children enjoy different types of play. Sometimes they have a strong preference; sometimes they like to switch around. Their preference is important for identifying and holding their interest. Our short video clips explain the Floortime steps of follow the lead, challenge, and expand with each type of play. With each you can:

  • Watch the video, which shows Dr. Greenspan working with a child on a type of play
  • Read the summary that explains techniques of the Floortime step and the type of play
  • Listen to the accompanying audio clip which expands on these ideas
  • Review the video for the subtle signs that keep the interaction flowing
  • Practice evaluating a Floortime session to help understand the Floortime steps.

For each type of play there is one example of Following the Lead. For Challenge/Expand there are either three or four examples for each. Choose one type of play and follow the Floortime steps. You can do the other two before or after you try your own Floortime session.
