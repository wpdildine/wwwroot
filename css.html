Welcome!

Welcome to the Floortime e-manual.
This interactive tool will provide you with all you need to get started with Floortime, troubleshoot challenges you may encounter, and explore possibilities for helping your child to grow. Perhaps more importantly, it will support you as you build closer emotional connections with your child.

Inside the e-Manual

The training course includes these six components. We ask that you follow the steps in this order to make sure that you have the background and understanding to use Floortime to its fullest. Our best wishes for joy in the new experiences with your child.

  • Start your training by learning about the three critical steps in doing Floortime.
  • Delve into short Floortime video clips by selecting one of three types of play based on your child’s abilities: sensory, object, or symbolic.
  • Explore videos with step-by-step explanations on how to help your child relate and communicate with you.
  • As you succeed, learn how to expand the play and your child’s abilities to communicate and think.
  • Set the scene for a Floortime session with your own child.
  • Get insights and tips to help with roadblocks to success
Get Started

The Steps to Successful Floortime

Floortime is play, but it is play with a purpose. With this training
course, you learn to tune into your child’s emotions and interests. By
responding to these basic motivations, you open a new world for you
and your child. Begin by following your child’s lead.

Once you’ve completed the initial introductory training, you’ll have a chance to go back and explore more types of play, suggestions for expanding play, and ideas for keeping Floortime fresh and engaging for both you and your child.
